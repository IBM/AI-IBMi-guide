Welcome to the guide for using AI with IBM i — a practical resource for developers, architects, and administrators who want to bring AI capabilities to IBM i workloads.

## What you'll find here

This guide is organized around the questions practitioners actually ask:

- **[Why AI with IBM i?](overview/WHY)** — The case for applying AI to your existing IBM i data and applications
- **[Choosing an AI Stack](overview/CHOOSING_STACK)** — A comparison of the platforms and tools available, from IBM-native to open source
- **[AI Hardware](overview/ai_hardware/WHY_POWER)** — How IBM Power hardware accelerates AI workloads on-premises
- **Use Cases** — Practical journeys for [Machine Learning](journeys/ML), [Deep Learning](journeys/DL), [Generative AI](journeys/GENAI), [Agentic AI](journeys/AGENTIC), and [Developer Experience](journeys/DEVX)
- **Integrations** — How to connect AI platforms to Db2 for i data, stream transactions, and call AI from IBM i applications
- **Solutions** — Profiles of specific products and platforms, with capability report cards

## Where to start

If you're new, start with **[Why AI with IBM i?](overview/WHY)** for context, then **[Choosing an AI Stack](overview/CHOOSING_STACK)** to understand your options.

If you have a specific use case in mind, jump directly to the relevant journey or integration page from the sidebar.
