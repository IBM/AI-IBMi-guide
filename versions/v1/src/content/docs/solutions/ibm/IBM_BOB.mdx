## What is IBM Bob?

<Image src={bobImage} alt="IBM Bob" width={110} height={110} style="float: right;" />

IBM Bob is an AI SDLC (Software Development Lifecycle) partner that augments your existing workflows and helps you work confidently with real codebases!

- **Specialized Modes**: Bob adapts to your specific needs with purpose-built modes that optimize behavior for different development scenarios.
- **Powerful Tools**: Bob comes with a comprehensive set of tools that extend capabilities beyond text generation.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: Extend Bob's capabilities with custom tools and integrations via MCP.
- **Agent Skills**: Provide Bob reusable instruction sets that teach Bob new workflows and specialized tasks.
- **Bob Rules**: Customize Bob's output with your specific preferences and project requirements for coding style, documentation approach, and decision-making processes.
- **Bob Shell**: Take Bob's capabilities beyond your editor with Bob Shell, bringing the same AI assistance to your terminal.
