The Wallaroo AI Starter Kit for IBM Power is designed to bridge the 
gap between AI development and enterprise deployment infrastructure. 
It empowers organizations to move beyond model training and effortlessly 
turn their valuable AI models into state-of-the-art AI Inferencing.

The Wallaroo AI Starter Kit is specifically optimized for the IBM Power
Architecture, making the most of features like Power’s MMA (Matrix Multiply 
Assist) and Spyre Technology. This powerful combination is engineered to 
deliver enterprise-grade performance and reliability.
